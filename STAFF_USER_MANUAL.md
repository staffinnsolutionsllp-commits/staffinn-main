# Staffinn – Staff User Manual
### For First-Time Staff Users | Simple English Guide

---

## Who Is This Manual For?

This manual is for anyone who wants to join Staffinn as a **Staff member** — meaning you are a skilled professional (electrician, developer, nurse, teacher, accountant, etc.) who wants to be found by recruiters and get hired through the platform.

---

## PART 1 — FIRST VISIT: WHAT YOU WILL SEE

When you open the Staffinn website for the first time, you will land on the **Home Page**.

### What is on the Home Page?

**Header (top bar):**
- Staffinn logo on the left — click it anytime to come back to this page
- Navigation links: **Staff | Institute | Recruiter | News**
- A **Sign In** button on the top right

**Hero Section (big banner):**
- A large background image with a search bar
- The search bar has 4 dropdowns: State, City, Sector, Role
- A **Search** button — use this to find other staff profiles
- If you search, a **Clear** button appears to reset results

**Below the banner you will see 3 sections:**
1. **Trending Staff** — cards of top-rated staff members on the platform
2. **Trending Jobs** — job listings posted by recruiters
3. **Trending Courses** — popular courses from institutes

**Footer** at the bottom with platform information.

> At this point you are just a visitor. To use any real feature, you need to register and log in.

---

## PART 2 — HOW TO REGISTER

### Step 1 — Open the Registration Form
- Click the **Sign In** button in the top-right corner of the header.
- A login popup will appear.
- On the **left side** of the popup you will see: "Don't have an account?"
- Click the **Register** button.

### Step 2 — Fill the Staff Registration Form
A form will open with these fields (all are required):

| Field | What to enter |
|-------|--------------|
| Full Name | Your complete real name |
| Email | Your active email address |
| Password | A strong password (mix of letters, numbers, symbols) |
| Confirm Password | Type the same password again |
| Phone Number | Your 10-digit mobile number |

**Form Quality Bar:** As you fill each field, a coloured progress bar appears at the top of the form. It turns green when all fields are filled correctly. This is just a visual guide — it does not block you.

**Password Strength Indicator:** Below the password field, a strength meter shows if your password is weak, medium, or strong. Use a strong password.

**Real-time Errors:** If you type something wrong (e.g. invalid email format), a red error message appears below that field immediately. Fix it before submitting.

### Step 3 — Submit
- Click the **Register** button at the bottom of the form.
- If registration is successful, the form closes and the **Login popup** opens automatically with your email pre-filled.
- You will see a message: *"Registration successful! Please sign in with your credentials."*

---

## PART 3 — HOW TO LOG IN

### Step 1 — Open Login
- Click **Sign In** in the header.
- The login popup opens.

### Step 2 — Enter Details
- **Email** — the email you registered with
- **Password** — your password
- Click the eye icon (👁️) next to the password field to show or hide your password.

### Step 3 — Submit
- Click the **Login** button.
- If correct, the popup closes and you are taken directly to your **Staff Dashboard** at `/dashboard/staff`.
- A small message "Complete your dashboard" appears briefly at the top — this reminds you to fill your profile.

### If Login Fails
- A red error toast appears: *"Invalid credentials. Please try again."*
- Check your email and password and try again.

---

## PART 4 — AFTER LOGIN: THE HEADER CHANGES

Once logged in, the header changes:
- The **Sign In** button is replaced by **My Profile**
- A **bell icon** (🔔) appears next to My Profile — this is your notification bell
- Click **My Profile** to open a dropdown with:
  - **My Dashboard** — goes to your staff dashboard
  - **Logout** — logs you out and returns to the home page

---

## PART 5 — YOUR STAFF DASHBOARD

Your dashboard is your personal control centre. Go to it by clicking **My Profile → My Dashboard**.

The dashboard has two parts:
1. **Left Sidebar** — navigation menu
2. **Right Content Area** — the page content changes based on what you click in the sidebar

---

### THE SIDEBAR

At the top of the sidebar you will see:
- Your **profile photo** (or your name's first letter if no photo)
- Your **name** and **email**
- A **mode badge** — shows either **"Active Staff"** or **"Seeker Mode"**

Below that are the navigation buttons:

| Button | What it does |
|--------|-------------|
| Dashboard | Overview of your stats (only visible in Active Staff mode) |
| My Profile | Edit your profile and manage files |
| Contact History | See all your messages and conversations |
| My Courses | View courses you have enrolled in |
| Government Schemes | Browse government skill and employment schemes |

On mobile, a **hamburger menu button** (three lines) appears at the top. Tap it to open or close the sidebar.

---

## PART 6 — MY PROFILE TAB (Most Important)

This is the first tab you see after login. It has two different views depending on your mode.

---

### THE MODE TOGGLE (Very Important)

At the top of the My Profile tab, there is a **Profile Mode** section with a toggle switch.

**Two modes:**

**Seeker Mode (toggle OFF — default)**
- Your profile is PRIVATE. No one can find you.
- You can browse jobs, courses, and hire other staff.
- You see a simplified profile form.

**Active Staff Mode (toggle ON)**
- Your profile is PUBLIC. Recruiters can find and contact you.
- You appear on the Staff page and Home page.
- You get a full detailed profile form to fill.
- Your Dashboard tab becomes visible.

**How to switch modes:**
1. Click the toggle switch.
2. If you are turning it ON for the first time, a popup appears saying **"Complete Your Profile"**.
3. The popup lists all mandatory fields you must fill.
4. Click **Complete Profile** in the popup — this takes you to the full profile form with the toggle already set to ON.
5. Fill all mandatory fields and click **Update and Go Live**.
6. Your profile is now live and visible to recruiters.

To go back to Seeker mode, just click the toggle again. Your profile becomes private instantly.

---

### SEEKER MODE — Profile Form

When the toggle is OFF, you see a simple form with two sections:

**Left side — Basic Information:**
- **Full Name** — edit your name
- **Email** — read-only, cannot be changed
- **Phone Number** — edit your phone
- **User ID** — read-only, your unique ID on the platform

**Change Password section:**
- **Current Password** — enter your existing password
- **New Password** — enter the new password you want
- **Confirm New Password** — type the new password again
- Click **Save Changes** to save. If you leave the password fields empty, only your name and phone are updated.

**Right side — Profile Photo:**
- Shows your current photo or your name's first letter
- Click **Upload Photo** to select an image from your device (image files only)
- If a photo exists, a **Remove** button appears to delete it

---

### ACTIVE STAFF MODE — Full Profile Form

When the toggle is ON, you see the complete profile form. This is what recruiters will see.

**Mandatory fields (marked with * — must be filled to go live):**
- Address (House No. / Street / Area)
- State (dropdown — select from all Indian states)
- City (dropdown — appears after you select a state)
- Pincode
- Sector (your industry, e.g. IT, Healthcare, Education)
- Role (appears after you select a sector, e.g. Software Developer, Nurse, Teacher)
- Skills (type your skills separated by commas, e.g. "Python, Communication, Excel")

**Other fields (optional but recommended):**
- Full Name
- Phone Number

**Experience Section:**
- Shows one experience entry by default with fields: Role/Position, Company, Salary, Start Date, End Date
- Click **+ Add Experience** to add more entries
- Click **Delete** on any entry to remove it (you must keep at least one)

**Education Section — three sub-sections:**

*10th Grade:*
- Percentage, Year of Completion, School Name

*12th Grade:*
- Percentage, Year of Completion, School Name

*Graduation:*
- Degree (e.g. B.Tech, B.Com)
- College/University name
- Percentage or CGPA
- Start Date
- Check **"Currently Pursuing"** if still studying — this hides the End Date field
- End Date (if not pursuing)

**Availability Status — choose one:**
- **Available** — you are ready for new work right now
- **Busy** — you are currently occupied
- **Part-time** — you are only available for part-time work

**Right side — Profile Photo, Resume, Certificates:**

*Profile Photo:*
- Click **Upload Photo** to upload an image
- Click **Remove** to delete it

*Resume:*
- Click **Upload Resume (PDF)** to upload your CV (PDF files only)
- Once uploaded, a **View Resume** link appears
- Recruiters with a Recruiter account can view and download your resume

*My Certificates:*
- Click **+ Add** to open the certificate upload form
- Fill: Course Name, Issuer/Institution, Duration, Issue Date
- Upload the certificate PDF
- Click **Add Certificate** to save
- Each certificate shows a **Delete** button to remove it

**Profile Completion Bar:**
- Shows a percentage (0–100%) based on 4 items: Name, Skills, Resume, Profile Photo
- A checklist below shows which items are done (✓) and which are missing

**Update and Go Live button:**
- Click this to save all your profile data AND make your profile public
- If any mandatory field is missing, an alert lists what needs to be filled
- On success, you are taken to the Dashboard tab

---

## PART 7 — DASHBOARD TAB (Active Staff Mode Only)

This tab is only visible when you are in Active Staff mode.

### Stats Cards
Two cards at the top:
- **Total Applications** — how many job applications you have submitted
- **Profile Views** — how many times recruiters have viewed your profile

### Application Trend Chart
A line chart showing your application activity over months. Helps you see when you were most active.

### Recent Applications Table
A table showing your latest job applications with columns:
- Company name
- Job title
- Date applied
- Status (e.g. Applied, Reviewed)

If you have no applications yet, it shows: *"No applications yet. Start applying for jobs!"*

### Recent Activity Feed
A list of recent events like profile views and job applications, with dates. Shows the 10 most recent activities.

---

## PART 8 — CONTACT HISTORY TAB

This is your messaging centre. It works in both Seeker and Active Staff modes.

### What you see:
**Left panel — Conversations list:**
- A search bar to find a specific person by name
- A **Refresh** button (circular arrow icon) to reload the list
- Each conversation shows: profile photo, name, user type badge (colour-coded), last message preview, time, and unread count badge

**Right panel — Chat window:**
- When you click a conversation on the left, the full chat opens on the right
- You can type and send messages
- If no conversation is selected, it shows: *"Select a conversation to start chatting"*

**Resizable divider:** You can drag the divider between the left and right panels to make one side wider.

The list refreshes automatically every 30 seconds.

---

## PART 9 — MY COURSES TAB

Shows all courses you have enrolled in.

### What you see:
Each enrolled course appears as a card showing:
- Course thumbnail image (if available)
- Course name
- Duration
- Instructor name
- Progress percentage (e.g. 45%)
- A progress bar (fills up as you complete content)
- **Continue Learning** button

### What "Continue Learning" does:
Clicking it takes you to the **Course Learning Page** for that course.

### If you have no courses:
It shows: *"You haven't enrolled in any courses yet. Visit institute pages to explore available courses."*

---

## PART 10 — COURSE LEARNING PAGE

When you click Continue Learning, you enter the full course page.

### Left side — Content area:
- **Video player** — when you click a video lesson, it plays here. When the video ends, it is automatically marked as complete.
- **Quiz area** — when you click a quiz, the questions appear here inline
- **Notes/Assignments** — downloadable PDF files from the course
- **Tab bar** at the bottom with three tabs:
  - **Overview** — course description, prerequisites, syllabus
  - **Notes** — downloadable course materials (View / Download buttons)
  - **Reviews** — course ratings and a form to write your own review (only if enrolled)

### Right side — Course content sidebar:
- Shows all modules (sections) of the course
- Click a module header to expand or collapse it
- Each module shows its lessons, quizzes, and assignments
- Icons show status:
  - 🔒 Lock icon — not enrolled
  - ▶ Play icon — video, not yet watched
  - Q — quiz
  - 📄 — assignment
  - ✓ Green check — completed

### Quizzes:
- Click a quiz item to open it
- Answer all questions (radio buttons)
- A progress bar shows how many questions you have answered
- Click **Submit Quiz** (only active when all questions are answered)
- Results show your score, pass/fail status, and correct answers
- If you pass (score ≥ passing score), the quiz is locked — you cannot retake it
- If you fail, a **Retry Quiz** button appears

### Enrolling in a course from this page:
If you are not yet enrolled, the right sidebar shows:
- Course fee (₹ amount)
- **Enroll Now** button

Clicking Enroll Now:
- **Free online course** — enrolls you immediately
- **Paid online course** — opens a Razorpay payment popup
- **On-campus paid course** — shows two options:
  - **Pay Here** — pay online via Razorpay
  - **Pay at Institute** — submit an enrollment request and pay when you visit the institute

---

## PART 11 — GOVERNMENT SCHEMES TAB

Shows government schemes for skill development and employment.

### What you see:
- A **search bar** — type any keyword to filter schemes
- A **category dropdown** — filter by scheme type
- Scheme cards showing: scheme name, description, date added, category badge
- Each card has a **View Scheme** button — clicking it opens the official government scheme link in a new tab

If no schemes match your search, it shows: *"No schemes found. Try adjusting your search or filter criteria."*

---

## PART 12 — BROWSING THE WEBSITE AS STAFF

Even after logging in, you can still browse all public pages.

### Staff Page (`/staff`)
- Browse all active staff profiles
- Use filters: State, City, Sector, Role, Skills (text input), Experience level, Availability, Minimum Rating
- Click **Get In Touch** on any staff card to view their full profile
- Inside the profile popup you can: Chat, Email, leave a Review, or Mark as Hired

### Jobs Page (`/jobs`)
- Browse all active job listings
- Filter by: job title/keyword, location, job type (full-time, part-time, internship, contract), experience level, salary range
- Click **Apply Now** on a job card — this takes you to the recruiter's page where you can apply
- Use **Reset all filters** to clear all filters at once
- Jobs are paginated (10 per page) — use the page numbers at the bottom to navigate

### Courses Page (`/courses`)
- Browse all available courses from all institutes
- Filter by: course name/institute name (search), category, mode (online, on-campus, offline, hybrid)
- Click **View Course** on any card to open the Course Learning Page

### Institutes Page (`/institute`)
- Browse registered training institutes
- Click an institute to view its full profile, courses, placements, and contact details

### News Page (`/news`)
- Read latest industry news and updates
- Posted by recruiters and admins

---

## PART 13 — NOTIFICATIONS

The **bell icon** (🔔) in the header shows your notifications.

- A red badge appears on the bell when you have unread notifications
- Click the bell to see the list
- Notifications include: profile view alerts, job application updates, course enrollment confirmations, and messages

---

## PART 14 — IF YOUR PROFILE IS HIDDEN

If an admin hides your profile, you will see:

1. A **red notification bar** at the top of your dashboard saying: *"You are hidden from Staffinn."*
2. A **Request Help** link in that bar — click it to open a help popup
3. The popup explains why you may be hidden and gives you options to contact support

While hidden, your profile does not appear in search results even if you are in Active Staff mode.

---

## PART 15 — LOGGING OUT

- Click **My Profile** in the header
- Click **Logout** in the dropdown
- You are logged out and taken back to the Home page

---

## QUICK REFERENCE — STEP BY STEP FOR A NEW STAFF USER

1. Visit the website → see the Home page
2. Click **Sign In** → click **Register** → fill the Staff form → click **Register**
3. Login popup opens → enter email and password → click **Login**
4. You are taken to your **Staff Dashboard**
5. Go to **My Profile** tab
6. Toggle the switch to **Active Staff** → popup appears → click **Complete Profile**
7. Fill all mandatory fields: Address, State, City, Pincode, Sector, Role, Skills
8. Upload your **Profile Photo** and **Resume (PDF)**
9. Add your **Experience** and **Education** details
10. Set your **Availability** status
11. Click **Update and Go Live** → your profile is now public
12. Go to **Dashboard** tab to track your profile views and applications
13. Use **My Courses** to enroll in and learn from courses
14. Use **Government Schemes** to find schemes you can benefit from
15. Use **Contact History** to chat with recruiters who contact you
16. Browse **Jobs** page to find and apply for jobs

---

## TIPS

- Always upload a clear profile photo — profiles with photos get more views
- Add as many skills as possible, separated by commas
- Keep your availability status updated so recruiters know if you are free
- Check your notifications regularly for recruiter messages
- If you only want to browse and hire others, stay in **Seeker mode** — your profile stays private
