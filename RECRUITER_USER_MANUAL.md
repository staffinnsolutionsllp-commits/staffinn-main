# Staffinn – Recruiter User Manual
### For First-Time Recruiters | Simple English Guide

---

## Who Is This Manual For?

This manual is for anyone who wants to join Staffinn as a **Recruiter** — meaning you represent a company or organisation and want to post jobs, find skilled staff, and hire candidates through the platform.

> Recruiters cannot self-register. You must send a registration request to Staffinn and wait for approval. This is explained in Part 2.

---

## PART 1 — FIRST VISIT: WHAT YOU WILL SEE

When you open the Staffinn website for the first time, you land on the **Home Page**.

### What is on the Home Page?

**Header (top bar):**
- Staffinn logo — click it anytime to return to the home page
- Navigation links: **Staff | Institute | Recruiter | News**
- A **Sign In** button on the top right

**Hero Section (big banner):**
- A large background image with a search bar
- Dropdowns: State, City, Sector, Role — use these to search for staff profiles
- A **Search** button and a **Clear** button (appears after searching)

**Below the banner:**
1. **Trending Staff** — top-rated staff profiles on the platform
2. **Trending Jobs** — most applied job listings
3. **Trending Courses** — popular courses from institutes

**Footer** at the bottom.

> You are a visitor at this point. To use recruiter features, you need to request registration and get approved.

---

## PART 2 — HOW TO REGISTER AS A RECRUITER

Recruiters cannot register directly. You must submit a request to Staffinn for approval.

### Step 1 — Open the Request Form
- Click **Sign In** in the top-right header.
- The login popup opens.
- On the left side, click **"Register as an Institute / Recruiter"**.

### Step 2 — Fill the Request Form
A form appears with these fields:

| Field | What to enter |
|-------|--------------|
| Select Type | Choose **Recruiter** from the dropdown |
| Name | Your company or your full name |
| Email | Your active business email |
| Phone Number | Your 10-digit contact number |

Click **Submit Request**.

### Step 3 — Wait for Approval
- After submitting, you will see: *"Registration request submitted successfully! You will receive an email once your request is approved."*
- Staffinn reviews your request.
- Once approved, you receive your **login credentials (email and password)** by email.

---

## PART 3 — HOW TO LOG IN

### Step 1 — Open Login
- Click **Sign In** in the header.

### Step 2 — Enter Credentials
- **Email** — the email you used in your request (or the one Staffinn sent credentials to)
- **Password** — the password sent to you by Staffinn
- Click the eye icon to show or hide your password.

### Step 3 — Submit
- Click **Login**.
- On success, you are taken directly to your **Recruiter Dashboard** at `/dashboard/recruiter`.
- A brief message "Complete your dashboard" appears at the top.

### If Login Fails
- A red error message appears: *"Invalid credentials. Please try again."*
- Check your email and password. Use the exact credentials sent by Staffinn.

---

## PART 4 — AFTER LOGIN: THE HEADER CHANGES

Once logged in:
- **Sign In** is replaced by **My Profile**
- A **bell icon** (🔔) appears — this is your notification bell
- Click **My Profile** to open a dropdown:
  - **My Dashboard** — goes to your recruiter dashboard
  - **Logout** — logs you out

---

## PART 5 — YOUR RECRUITER DASHBOARD

Your dashboard is your complete hiring control centre. It has:
1. **Left Sidebar** — navigation menu
2. **Right Content Area** — changes based on what you click

---

### THE SIDEBAR

At the top of the sidebar:
- Your **company logo / profile photo** (or first letter of company name if no photo)
- Your **company name**

Below that, the navigation menu:

| Menu Item | What it does |
|-----------|-------------|
| My Profile | Edit your company profile and go live |
| Dashboard Overview | See stats, charts, and recent jobs |
| Job Management | Post, edit, and delete job listings |
| Candidate Search | View and manage job applicants |
| Hiring History | See all candidates you have hired |
| Institutes | View institutes that applied to your jobs |
| News | Post and manage company news/events |
| Contact History | Your messages and conversations |
| Government Schemes | Browse government employment schemes |
| HRMS | Opens the Staffinn HRMS system in a new tab |

On mobile, a **hamburger button** (three lines) appears at the top to open/close the sidebar.

---

## PART 6 — MY PROFILE TAB

This is where you set up your public company profile. Everything you fill here appears on your public Recruiter page that candidates and staff can see.

> Important: Changes only go live after you click **"Update Profile & Go Live"** at the bottom.

### Profile Photo
- Click **Upload Photo** to select a company logo or photo (image files, max 5MB)
- A preview appears immediately after upload — it shows as "Pending" until you click Update Profile & Go Live
- Click **Remove Photo** to delete the current photo

### Company Information
| Field | What to enter |
|-------|--------------|
| Company Name * | Your company's full name |
| Industry * | Your industry (e.g. Technology, Healthcare) |
| Location * | Your company's city/address |
| Pincode | Your area pincode |
| Website | Your company website URL (e.g. https://yourcompany.com) |
| Company Description * | A paragraph describing your company |

### Recruiter Information
| Field | What to enter |
|-------|--------------|
| Email | Read-only — your login email, cannot be changed |
| Recruiter Name * | Your full name |
| Designation * | Your job title (e.g. HR Manager, Talent Acquisition Lead) |
| Hiring Experience * | How many years you have been hiring (e.g. 5+ years) |
| Phone | Your contact number |

### Perks & Benefits
- Shows a list of benefits your company offers (e.g. Health insurance, Work from home)
- Click **+ Add Perk** to add a new benefit — type the benefit description in the text box
- Click **Remove** next to any perk to delete it

### Hiring Process
- Shows the steps in your hiring process (e.g. Online Application → HR Call → Technical Test → Offer)
- Each step has a **Title** and **Description**
- Click **+ Add Step** to add a new step
- Click **Remove** on any step to delete it
- Steps are numbered automatically

### Office Images
- Upload photos of your office/workplace to show candidates what your environment looks like
- Click **+ Add Image** to upload an image
- Uploaded images show as "Pending" until you save
- Click **Remove** on any image to delete it

### Common Interview Questions
- Add the questions you typically ask candidates in interviews
- Click **+ Add Question** to add a new question
- Click **Remove** to delete a question

### Change Password
- **Current Password** — enter your existing password
- **New Password** — enter the new password (minimum 6 characters)
- Click **Change Password** — this saves immediately without needing to click Update Profile & Go Live

### Update Profile & Go Live button
- Click this to save ALL profile changes and make them visible publicly on the Recruiter page
- On success: *"Profile updated successfully! Your changes are now live on the main recruiters page."*

## PART 7 — DASHBOARD OVERVIEW TAB

This is your analytics and summary page.

### 4 Metric Cards at the top:
| Card | What it shows |
|------|--------------|
| Jobs Posted | Total number of jobs you have posted |
| Applications | Total applications received across all jobs |
| Hires | Total candidates you have hired |
| Followers | Total followers of your recruiter profile |

Each card also shows how many were added this month.

### Charts Section
Two charts with date filter controls:
- **Year selector** — choose which year to view
- **From / To month selectors** — choose the month range

**Applications & Hiring Trends (Bar Chart):**
- Purple bars = number of applications per month
- Yellow bars = number of hires per month
- Hover over any bar to see exact numbers

**Hiring Funnel (Pie Chart):**
- Shows the ratio of total applications vs total hires
- Helps you understand your conversion rate

### Recent Job Postings Table
Shows your 3 most recent job postings with columns:
- Job Title, Department, Location, Graduation Year, Applications count, Status, Posted date
- **Edit** button — opens the job edit form
- **Delete** button — permanently removes the job (asks for confirmation first)

---

## PART 8 — JOB MANAGEMENT TAB

This is where you create and manage all your job listings.

### Posting a New Job
1. Click **+ Post New Job** button at the top
2. A form popup opens. Fill in all required fields (*):

| Field | What to enter |
|-------|--------------|
| Job Title * | e.g. Senior Frontend Developer |
| Department * | Select from: Engineering, Product, Design, Marketing, Sales, Analytics, HR, Finance |
| Job Type * | Full-time, Part-time, Contract, Freelance, or Internship |
| Experience Required * | e.g. `3-5` for 3 to 5 years, or `2` for 2 years |
| Salary Range * | e.g. ₹12-18 LPA |
| Location * | e.g. Bangalore (Hybrid) |
| Graduation Year | e.g. 2024 or 2023-2025 (optional) |
| Date Posted * | Auto-filled with today's date, can be changed |
| Status * | Active (visible), Closed (not accepting), or Draft (hidden) |
| Required Skills * | Comma-separated, e.g. React, Node.js, MongoDB |
| Job Description * | Full description of the role and responsibilities |

3. Click **Post Job** — the job is immediately visible on the public Jobs page and your Recruiter page.
4. Click **Cancel** to close without saving.

### Filtering Your Jobs
- **Search box** — type a job title, department, or location
- **Status dropdown** — filter by Active, Closed, or Draft
- **Department dropdown** — filter by department

### Editing a Job
- Click **Edit** next to any job in the table
- The same form opens with all existing data pre-filled
- Make your changes and click **Update Job**

### Deleting a Job
- Click **Delete** next to any job
- A confirmation popup asks: *"Are you sure you want to delete this job posting?"*
- Click OK to confirm — the job is removed from the public page immediately

---

## PART 9 — CANDIDATE SEARCH TAB

This shows all candidates (staff members) who have applied to your job postings.

### Filters at the top:
- **Search box** — type a candidate's name, skills, or experience (searches as you type with a small delay)
- **Status dropdown** — filter by: All, Hired, Rejected, New
- **Select Job dropdown** — filter candidates by which specific job they applied to

### Candidates Table
Each row shows:
- Name, Position applied for, Skills, Status badge, Applied Date
- **View Profile** — opens a detailed popup showing the candidate's full profile including skills, work experience, education, certificates, and a Download Resume button
- **Hired** — marks the candidate as hired (asks for confirmation). Updates their status and adds them to Hiring History
- **Delete** — marks the candidate as rejected (asks for confirmation). Removes them from the active list

### Candidate Profile Popup
When you click View Profile, a popup shows:
- Profile photo, name, profession, rating
- Location, phone, email, availability status
- Skills list
- Work experience (role, company, dates, salary)
- Education (10th, 12th, graduation details)
- Certificates with View Certificate links
- Resume with a **📥 Download** button

---

## PART 10 — HIRING HISTORY TAB

Shows a record of all candidates you have hired.

### What you see:
A table with columns:
- Name, Position, Hire Date
- **View Details** button — opens the candidate's full profile popup

If no one has been hired yet, it shows: *"No hiring records found. Hire candidates from the Candidate Search section."*

---

## PART 11 — INSTITUTES TAB

This shows all training institutes that have applied to your job postings on behalf of their students.

### Filter at the top:
- **All Applied Jobs dropdown** — filter institutes by which job they applied to

### Institute Cards
Each card shows:
- Institute name
- Contact details: phone, email, website
- **Applied Jobs** section — lists each job the institute applied to, with the date
- For each job: a **View Students** button

### View Students button
Clicking this opens a **Students Modal** showing all students from that institute who applied for that specific job.

**Students table columns:**
- Name (with a green "✓ Staffinn Verified" badge if the student is MIS-verified)
- Email, Qualification, Center/Specialization, Course/Expected Year
- **✅ Hired** button — hire this student (asks for confirmation, then removes them from the list)
- **❌ Rejected** button — reject this student (asks for confirmation, then removes them from the list)
- Skills (shows first 2 skills, +N for more)
- **View Profile** button — opens the student's full profile with all documents

**📥 Download All Students Detail button** (at the bottom of the modal):
- Downloads an Excel file (.xlsx) with complete details of all students
- File includes: name, email, phone, DOB, gender, address, degree, skills, resume URL, certificates, and more
- Filename format: `InstituteName_JobTitle_Students_Date.xlsx`

### View Details button (on institute card)
Opens the institute's full public profile page in a new browser tab.

### 📋 Hiring History button (on institute card)
Shows the hiring history specifically for that institute — all students you have hired or rejected from them.

---

## PART 12 — NEWS TAB

Post company news, job fairs, events, and announcements that appear on the public News page.

### Adding News
1. Click **+ Add News** button
2. A form popup opens:

| Field | What to enter |
|-------|--------------|
| News Title * | e.g. "Walk-in Drive – 15th August" |
| Date * | The date of the news or event |
| Venue | Where the event is happening (optional) |
| Expected Participants | How many people are expected (optional) |
| Banner Image | Upload an image for the news card (optional) |
| News Details * | Full description of the news or event |
| Mark as Verified | Check this box if the news is officially verified |

3. Click **Add News** — the news appears on the public News page immediately.

### News Cards
Each news item shows as a card with:
- Banner image (if uploaded)
- Title, company name, date, venue, expected participants, details
- A green **✓ Verified** badge if marked as verified
- **View** button — opens a full-screen view of the news
- **Edit** button — opens the form with existing data to edit
- **Delete** button — permanently deletes the news (asks for confirmation)

### Editing News
- Click **Edit** on any news card
- The form opens with all existing data pre-filled
- Make changes and click **Update News**

### Viewing News
- Click **View** to open a popup showing the full news with banner image, all details, and an **Edit News** button

---

## PART 13 — CONTACT HISTORY TAB

Your full messaging centre for conversations with staff, institutes, and other users.

### What you see:
**Left panel — Conversations list:**
- Search bar to find a person by name
- **Refresh** button (circular arrow) to reload the list
- Each conversation shows: profile photo, name, user type badge (colour-coded), last message preview, time, unread count

**Right panel — Chat window:**
- Click any conversation on the left to open the full chat on the right
- Type and send messages
- If no conversation is selected: *"Select a conversation to start chatting"*

**Resizable divider:** Drag the divider between left and right panels to resize them.

The list auto-refreshes every 30 seconds.

---

## PART 14 — GOVERNMENT SCHEMES TAB

Browse government schemes related to employment and skill development.

### What you see:
- **Search bar** — type any keyword to filter schemes
- **Category dropdown** — filter by scheme type
- Scheme cards showing: scheme name, description, date added, category badge
- **View Scheme** button on each card — opens the official government scheme link in a new tab

---

## PART 15 — HRMS (Human Resource Management System)

Clicking **HRMS** in the sidebar opens the Staffinn HRMS system in a **new browser tab**.

The HRMS is a separate system for managing your employees, attendance, payroll, and HR operations. Your recruiter ID is automatically passed to the HRMS so you are logged in as the correct company.

---

## PART 16 — NOTIFICATIONS

The **bell icon** (🔔) in the header shows your notifications.
- A red badge appears when you have unread notifications
- Click the bell to see the list
- Notifications include: new job applications, candidate status updates, institute applications, and messages

---

## PART 17 — IF YOUR PROFILE IS HIDDEN

If Staffinn admin hides your profile:
- A **red bar** appears at the top of your dashboard: *"You are hidden from Staffinn."*
- Click **Request Help** in that bar to open a help popup
- While hidden, your company profile and jobs do not appear publicly

---

## PART 18 — LOGGING OUT

- Click **My Profile** in the header
- Click **Logout**
- You are logged out and taken back to the Home page

---

## PART 19 — YOUR PUBLIC RECRUITER PAGE

Once your profile is live, anyone visiting the Recruiter page (`/recruiter`) can see your company card. Clicking it opens your full public profile showing:
- Company logo, name, industry, location, website
- Company description
- Active job listings with Apply Now buttons
- Perks & Benefits
- Hiring Process steps
- Common Interview Questions
- Office images

Staff members and seekers can apply to your jobs directly from this page.

---

## QUICK REFERENCE — STEP BY STEP FOR A NEW RECRUITER

1. Visit the website → click **Sign In** → click **"Register as an Institute / Recruiter"**
2. Select **Recruiter**, fill your name, email, phone → click **Submit Request**
3. Wait for Staffinn approval → receive login credentials by email
4. Login with your credentials → you are taken to your **Recruiter Dashboard**
5. Go to **My Profile** tab → fill all company and recruiter details
6. Upload your **company logo/photo**
7. Add your **Perks**, **Hiring Process steps**, **Office Images**, and **Interview Questions**
8. Click **Update Profile & Go Live** → your profile is now public
9. Go to **Job Management** → click **+ Post New Job** → fill job details → click **Post Job**
10. Wait for candidates to apply → check **Candidate Search** tab
11. Click **View Profile** to review a candidate's resume and details
12. Click **Hired** to hire or **Delete** to reject a candidate
13. Check **Institutes** tab to see students from training institutes who applied
14. Use **View Students** to review institute students and hire/reject them
15. Download student data using **📥 Download All Students Detail**
16. Post company news and events using the **News** tab
17. Use **Contact History** to chat with candidates and institutes
18. Access **HRMS** for employee management

---

## TIPS

- Always upload a company logo — profiles with logos get more trust from candidates
- Keep your job status updated — close jobs that are no longer accepting applications
- Use the Graduation Year field in jobs to target fresh graduates from specific batches
- Check the Institutes tab regularly — institutes send batches of pre-screened students
- Download the Excel file from the Students modal to review all student data offline
- Post news about job fairs and walk-in drives to attract more candidates
