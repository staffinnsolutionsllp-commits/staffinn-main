# Staffinn – Seeker User Manual

## What is a Seeker?
A Seeker is a logged-in user who browses and hires staff, applies for jobs, and enrolls in courses. Every registered user starts in **Seeker mode** by default.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### Register
1. Click **Register** on the homepage.
2. Fill in your name, email, and password.
3. Verify your email via the OTP sent to your inbox.
4. You are now logged in as a Seeker.

### Login
Click **Login**, enter your email and password, and submit.

---

## 2. Main Pages

### Home
The landing page. Shows platform highlights, featured jobs, courses, and news. Use the navigation bar to go to any section.

### Jobs Page (`/jobs`)
- Browse all active job listings posted by recruiters.
- Use filters (location, sector, role, experience) to narrow results.
- Click a job card to see full details.
- Click **Apply** to submit your application. You need to be logged in.
- Track your applications under **My Applications** in your dashboard.

### Courses Page (`/courses`)
- Browse available training courses.
- Click a course card to view details (syllabus, duration, fees, institute).
- Click **Enroll** to join a course.
- Pay online (Razorpay) or choose **Pay at Institute**.
- Access enrolled courses from your dashboard → **My Courses**.

### Staff Page (`/staff`)
- Browse profiles of skilled professionals available for hire.
- Use filters: State, City, Sector, Role, Skills, Experience, Availability, Rating.
- Click **Get In Touch** on any staff card to open their full profile.
- Inside the profile modal you can:
  - View profile details, skills, experience, and education.
  - **Chat** – send a direct message.
  - **Email** – opens your email client.
  - **WhatsApp** – available for recruiter accounts.
  - **Submit a Review** – rate the staff member (1–5 stars).
  - **Mark as Hired** – after contacting, confirm you hired them.

### Institutes Page (`/institutes`)
Browse registered training institutes, view their courses, placements, and contact details.

### News Page (`/news`)
Read the latest industry news and updates posted by recruiters and admins.

---

## 3. Your Dashboard (`/dashboard`)

After login, click your name or **Dashboard** in the header.

### Sidebar Tabs

| Tab | What it does |
|-----|-------------|
| **My Profile** | Edit your basic info, photo, and password |
| **Contact History** | See all staff members you have contacted |
| **My Courses** | View enrolled courses and learning progress |
| **Government Schemes** | Browse government skill-development schemes |

### My Profile (Seeker Mode)
- Edit **Full Name** and **Phone Number**.
- **Email** and **User ID** are read-only.
- **Change Password** – enter current password, then new password twice.
- Upload or remove your **Profile Photo** (image files only).
- Click **Save Changes** to save.

### Switching to Active Staff Mode
If you want to offer your own services as a professional:
1. Go to **My Profile**.
2. Toggle the **Profile Mode** switch from *Seeker* to *Active Staff*.
3. A popup will list mandatory fields to complete (Address, State, City, Pincode, Sector, Role, Skills).
4. Fill all fields, then click **Update and Go Live**.
5. Your profile becomes publicly visible to recruiters.

> Toggle it back to **Seeker** at any time to hide your profile.

### My Courses
- Lists all courses you have enrolled in.
- Shows progress, quiz scores, and completion status.
- Click a course to continue learning (videos, quizzes, assignments).

### Government Schemes
- Browse central and state government schemes for employment and skill development.
- Click a scheme card to read eligibility and application details.

### Contact History
- Shows a log of every staff member you contacted (call, email, WhatsApp, chat).
- Includes contact date and method.

---

## 4. Notifications
- The **bell icon** in the header shows unread notifications.
- Notifications include application status updates, course enrollment confirmations, and messages.

---

## 5. Messaging
- Click the **chat icon** on any staff profile or use the message center.
- Compose and send messages directly to staff members or recruiters.

---

## 6. Tips
- Complete your profile photo to make your account look trustworthy when you switch to Active Staff mode.
- Use specific skill keywords in the Staff page search for better results.
- Check **Government Schemes** regularly — new schemes are added by admins.
